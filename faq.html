Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy used records?
Yes! Bring in your vinyl and we'll evaluate it for cash or store credit.

Do you take special orders?
Absolutely. If we don't have it in stock, we'll do our best to order it.

Can I listen before buying?
Yes! We have a listening station available during business hours.

Do you sell turntables or gear?
Yes! We carry beginner-friendly setups, needles, sleeves, and cleaning kits.

What is your return policy?
New vinyl: exchange-only for manufacturer defects. Used vinyl: all sales final.
