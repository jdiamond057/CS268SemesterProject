Abraxas Wax

Our Story

Abraxas Wax began as a small passion project among Eau Claire music lovers. What started with a few crates of classic rock LPs has grown into one of the city's favorite stops for vinyl collectors, casual listeners, and curious newcomers.

Why Vinyl?

We believe in the warmth, character, and soul of analog sound. Vinyl invites us to slow down, appreciate the music, and rediscover the experience of listening with intention.

Our Mission

To share the magic of physical music, support the local arts scene, and provide a welcoming space for everyone. From seasoned crate diggers to students finding their first record.

Visit the Shop

Stop by to browse new releases, rare finds, local artists, and staff-picked favorites. We're proud to be part of Eau Claire's creative community.
